# Privacy Policy — SujoyDev

**Effective date:** 5 July 2026
**App:** SujoyDev (`com.sujoydev.app`)
**Developer:** Sujoy Ghoshal · Contact: sujoyghshal.s@gmail.com · +91 8927673775

This Privacy Policy explains what information the SujoyDev app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

## 1. Information We Collect

**Provided by you (only when you choose to):**
- **Google Sign-In:** your name, email address and profile photo, used solely to create your session and pre-fill forms. You may instead use Guest mode, which stores no identity with us.
- **Project requests / Hire Me forms:** name, company (optional), email, phone number, budget, timeline and project description — submitted so we can respond to your enquiry.
- **Bug reports:** the title and description you write, plus your device model, Android OS version and app version, attached automatically to help diagnose the issue.

**Collected automatically:**
- Basic technical information required for the app to function (device type, OS version). The app does **not** collect location data, contacts, advertising identifiers, or analytics profiles, and shows **no third-party advertising**.

## 2. How We Use Information

- To respond to project requests and hire enquiries.
- To investigate and fix reported bugs.
- To maintain your session, favorites and preferences (stored **locally on your device**).
- To send status notifications about your requests, if notifications are enabled.

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- Favorites, bookmarks, theme preference and queued submissions are stored locally on your device and can be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling.
- Submitted requests are transmitted over HTTPS to our server and stored in a secured database with access restricted to the developer.
- We retain enquiry data only as long as needed to handle your request and any resulting project.

## 4. Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties. Data is disclosed only if required by law.

## 5. Third-Party Services

- **Google Sign-In** (Google LLC) — authentication only. Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 6. Your Rights

You may request a copy or deletion of any data you have submitted by emailing **sujoyghshal.s@gmail.com** from the address used in the submission. Deletion requests are honoured within 30 days.

## 7. Children

The app is intended for users 18 and older and does not knowingly collect data from children.
